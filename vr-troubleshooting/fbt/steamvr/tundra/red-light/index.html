Tundra Trackers - Persistent Red Light

Let the tracker run out of battery.

This is purportedly caused by shutting down steamVR while the tracker is plugged in. There's nothing you can do about it aside from letting the tracker run completely out of charge (which will take many hours). After this, charge it up, and it will work fine again. (You can't even disconnect the battery, because it's soldered on)
