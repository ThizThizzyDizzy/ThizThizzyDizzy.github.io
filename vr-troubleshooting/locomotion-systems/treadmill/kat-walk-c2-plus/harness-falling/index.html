VR Troubleshooting

Harness falls down

Hook the harness cable back on its pulley.

Remove the back panel. There's a pulley near the top underneath the back panel. The harness cable may have slipped off; If you can, try hooking the cable back onto the pulley. (This is quite difficult to reach)
