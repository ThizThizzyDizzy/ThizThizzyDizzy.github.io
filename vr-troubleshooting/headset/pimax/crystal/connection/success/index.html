Pimax Crystal - Connection Problems

Restart the Pimax Service

Press "Restart Service" in pimax play. (VR Manager will do this for you automatically)

Manually restart the headset

Hold the power button until both the main headset light and the battery light turn off, and wait up to 2 minutes for the headset to restart and reconnect.
