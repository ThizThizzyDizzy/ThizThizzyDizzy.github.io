VR Troubleshooting (WIP)

Pimax Error 10900

The headset is connected to the integrated graphics card interface. The graphics card is too old to support direct mode. Also could be graphics card driver is also outdated, lacking support for direct mode.

Solution

Connect the headset to the dedicated graphics card
Update or downgrade the graphics card driver to different version
Check the graphics card model and ensure it meets the minimum requirements
