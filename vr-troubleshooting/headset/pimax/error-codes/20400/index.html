Pimax Error 20400

Failed to acquire the headset device

Solution

Restart the Pimax Play client, reinstall the Pimax Play client

Source: Pimax

Update pimax play

This error can be caused by using a version of pimax play that is too old. Update to the latest version from the Pimax website
